1. Human Resource Recruitment and Reverse Recruitment
Job Application Support:
  • Provide comprehensive job application support, including writing and editing resumes, cover letters, and developing LinkedIn profiles to effectively highlight clients' skills and experiences.

Candidate Screening and Interview Coordination:
  • Write job descriptions (JDs) tailored to the client's requirements and post them on job boards like LinkedIn, Indeed, and others.

  • Coordinate interviews between candidates and hiring managers, ensuring all parties are well prepared.

  • Maintenance of comprehensive personnel records, including employee personal information, employment history, performance evaluations, and training records.
  • Ensuring confidentiality and security of personnel records, implementing measures to protect sensitive employee information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

  • Regular audits and reviews of personnel records to ensure accuracy, completeness, and compliance with regulatory requirements, making any necessary updates or corrections as needed.

    HR Consulting for Small Businesses

  • Providing customized HR solutions tailored to the specific needs and challenges of small businesses, including recruitment, employee relations, and compliance.
  • Assisting small businesses in developing HR policies and procedures that align with legal requirements and industry best practices, ensuring consistency and fairness in workforce management.
  • Helping small businesses navigate complex HR regulations and compliance requirements, including employment laws, workplace safety regulations, and employee benefits administration.
  • Offering strategic HR guidance and advice to small business owners and managers, helping them optimize their HR practices to support business growth, improve employee engagement, and enhance organizational effectiveness.
